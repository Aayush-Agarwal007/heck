Frequently Asked Questions

Is Help Nexus available across all of India?

Yes! Help Nexus operates across all 28 states and 8 union territories of India. We partner with local emergency services, hospitals, and police departments to ensure seamless coverage in urban and rural areas.

What are the main emergency numbers to use?

In India, use 112 (unified emergency number) or the specific numbers: 100 (Police), 101 (Fire), 102 (Ambulance). Our app integrates with all these services for quick response. You can also use Help Nexus directly for faster coordination.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We use military-grade encryption (AES-256) and comply with India's Personal Data Protection Bill and other data protection regulations. Your privacy is our highest priority.

Does the app work with Indian hospitals?

Yes! We partner with major hospital chains including Apollo, Max Healthcare, Fortis, and government medical colleges across India. The app provides direct connections to nearby hospitals and available beds.

Is Help Nexus completely free to use?

Yes! Emergency assistance through Help Nexus is completely free for all Indian citizens. We also offer premium features like health tracking and family monitoring at affordable subscription prices.
